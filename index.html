Our Mission

The sky is the limit with virtual & augmented reality technology, and we're here to help you soar. All are welcome, from beginners to experienced makers. Understand the technology behind the magic and acquire the tools to build novel augmented and virtual reality experiences within a supportive community. Make important connections and gain access to venture capitalists and AR/VR employers.

We can't wait to see what you will build.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the expected cost for the academy series?

Our goal with this series is to keep the series as accessible as possible. We are currently working on getting sponsors to keep our costs low for attendees, and were able to launch our pilot series completely free to attendees. While we aren't able to guarantee that future courses will be offered for free, a large part of our mission is to create affordable educational materials and initiatives to help bring VR and AR to everyone.

Q: Where will this be held?

We are currently starting out in the San Francisco Bay Area, with the exact location of courses TBD as we get closer to the start of the series. We are looking to expand globally as quickly as we can to establish a network worldwide of similar initiatives to teach the fundamentals of immersive technologies to aspiring professionals in the AR/VR industry. We encourage you to join the interest list regardless of location, to stay updated as we work on bringing the courses to a city near you.

Q: When will the next series of classes begin?

As we get closer to Fall 2016, we'll have more to share on the course details. Join the interest list to make sure you're getting the latest updates from us!
