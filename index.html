Our Mission

ARVR Academy is a nonprofit group dedicated to diversity and inclusion in AR and VR through education and professional development efforts to make the ARVR industry more accessible to people of all backgrounds. We seek to increase more representation of people from underrepresented communities inclusive of entrepreneurial, technical, and creative roles in the industry. We are working towards an open sourced curriculum of immersive technologies, and aim to help anyone, regardless of prior experience or background, find their spot in the growing AR and VR industries.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the expected cost for the academy series?

Our goal with this series is to keep the series as accessible as possible. Our current plan is to charge a fee of $120 for a six class series, but we do also offer partial and full scholarship options for those who are unable to cover the cost of the labs.

Q: What prerequisite knowledge do I need to have?

We firmly believe that all backgrounds are relevant to the fields of AR and VR. No prerequisite knowledge is needed, though participants may enjoy reading about 3D modeling or game development prior to attending.

Q: Where will this be held?

We are starting out in the San Francisco Bay Area with labs run in San Francisco and the South Bay, and for Fall 2016, we will also be hosting classes in Los Angeles. We are looking to expand globally as quickly as we can to establish a network worldwide of similar initiatives to teach the fundamentals of immersive technologies to aspiring professionals in the AR/VR industry. We encourage you to join the interest list regardless of location, to stay updated as we work on bringing the courses to a city near you. If you or an organization you know is interested in hosting introductory courses in your city, please reach out and let us know!

Q: When will the next series of classes begin?

Dates vary by location, but our first Fall series will kick off on September 12th in San Francisco. We are currently accepting applications for our SF and LA classes here!
