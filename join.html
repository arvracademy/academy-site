Get Involved

As a non-profit organization, there are many ways you can help us with our mission, including, but not limited to:

  • Start a class series in your country/city. Ideally, we need two people to run a new location - at least one of whom has some Unity or VR experience. If you’re interested, please e-mail us and we will help get you up and running.


  • Volunteer with us! You can volunteer your time to help in other ways. We run a lot of events and do a lot of community outreach, and are always in need of people who are enthusiastic and hardworking. As a bonus, you get priority access to events and an amazing network of people who will support you in your career.


  • Give. The more money we have, the bigger our impact. Cash donations are great, but we are also interested in donations of things like Unity-ready Laptops (we loan these out to students who don’t have access to a laptop for classes), HMDs of all kinds - we’re building a library of headsets for loan to students and other local developers, or locations, food or drink donations for events. We are also interested in conference ticket sponsorships, or scholarships for low-income students. The lower we can make the cost of our classes, the better.



We need visibility to increase our impact on the industry. Reach to us if you have an event that has no diverse speakers, if you want to get involved, or if you are interested in attending our events!
